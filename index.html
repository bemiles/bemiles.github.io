Research Interests

I primarily work with mid-infrared spectroscopy to characterize the atmospheres of brown dwarfs and gas giant exoplanets. I want to improve ground-based mid-infrared instruments to characterize existing exoplanets and find Earth-like planets. I am currently a postdoc at the University of Arizona in Professor Daniel Apai's group.
