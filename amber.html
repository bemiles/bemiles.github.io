Amber Radiance 1 Camera Images

Someone found a very old Amber Radiance 1 staring camera, there is very little documentation on it, and I've been pointing it at various things to see how it works. The camera is sensitive from 3 to 5 microns, cools itself on closed-loop system, and produces video from readouts on its InSb array. Below is an image of a field at UCSC and an image of the half full Moon.
