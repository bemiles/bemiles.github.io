Dr. Brittany E. Miles

I am an assistant professor at Steward Observatory within the University of Arizona. I use mid-infrared spectroscopy to characterize the atmospheres of brown dwarfs and exoplanets. I want to improve ground-based mid-infrared instruments to characterize existing exoplanets and find Earth-like planets.
