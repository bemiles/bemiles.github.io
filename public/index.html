Dr. Brittany E. Miles

I use mid-infrared spectroscopy to characterize the atmospheres of brown dwarfs and exoplanets. I want to improve ground-based mid-infrared instruments to characterize existing exoplanets and find Earth-like planets. I am currently a 51 Pegasi b and Presidential Postdoctoral Fellow at the University of Arizona. Fall 2025 I will join the faculty at Steward Observatory as an assistant professor.
