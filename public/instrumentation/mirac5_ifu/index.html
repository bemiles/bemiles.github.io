Overview

The fifth iteration of the MIRAC (Mid-Infrared Array Camera) instrument is an imager that can capture light between 3 - 13 microns. MIRAC-5 will leverage an annular groove phase mask coronagraph and the MMT adaptive optics system to characterize directly imaged exoplanets and circumstellar disks. We will upgrade MIRAC-5 to have a low resolution N-band (7.5 -13 micron) IFU mode. This instrument upgrade will be a technical demonstration for TEMPO.

I have funding for a graduate student to work on this project.
